## Scope Note

This skill is grounded in public rules, public studies, and public toolchains. It does not claim that any specific wording guarantees score improvement, and it does not claim to have manually validated hundreds of individual "negative-to-positive" score-flip cases. Instead, it follows patterns supported by public datasets and retrospective analyses.
